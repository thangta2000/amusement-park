ADVENTURES CLUB

Adventurers Club is in Plaça Tibidabo in front of the main entrance, serving a wide range of salads, tapas and sandwiches. It has a terrace, indoor dining room with air conditioning and Wi-Fi.

Opening times: from 10am until the Park and/or Panorcamic Area closes.


Also, from 10.30 am to 11.30 am, enjoy its breakfast offer for only €3,50: toasted cheese and ham sandwich + coffee or coffee with milk (only available when the amusement park opens).
